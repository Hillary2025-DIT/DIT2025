UNIVERSITY GUILD GOVERNMENT

The Students Guild is a link between the students' body and the University Administration. " It is headed by a Guild President who is democratically elected by the students. This leadership structure ensures that student voices are heard and represented at the highest levels of university administration .
The guild government is significant in several ways:
Leadership Development:The Guild provides opportunities for students to develop leadership skills, and encourage student leaders to "aim for transformational change" and make a meaningful impact within the university community.
Student Representation: The guild government represents the interests of students and ensures their voices are heard by the university administration.
Community Building: The guild government organizes events and activities that promote community building and enhance student life, such as the annual Guild Run, which aims to raise funds for students facing financial challenges.
Promoting Student Welfare: The guild government works to address student concerns and promote student welfare, as evident in the university's efforts to improve infrastructure and facilities, such as renovating halls of residence and upgrading roads
