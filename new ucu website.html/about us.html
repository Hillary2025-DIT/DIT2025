ABOUT US

Located in the heart of Arua District, Uganda Christian University Arua Campus is a vibrant community of scholars and leaders dedicated to academic excellence, spiritual growth, and community service. Established in 1959 by the African Inland Mission, the campus has a rich history of training lay readers and offering theological education in West Nile Region. In 2003, the institution became part of the Uganda Christian University family that was established by the church of Uganda in 1997 in response to a call for quality university Education with a christian Perspective., expanding its academic programs and growing into a thriving campus.From its humble beginnings as a Rural Trade School, the campus evolved into a lay readers training college, providing theological education to the community. After joining UCU family, the campus expanded its academic offerings, introducing programs in Business Administration, Education,information Technology and Social Sciences. Today, the campus continue to build on this legacy, providing quality education that transforms lives and communities.
